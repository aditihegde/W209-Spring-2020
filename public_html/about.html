Goal of the Viz

Using the vizualizations, the idea is to educate and share with the users in the United States how global warming impacts them, their community and their livelihood. These tasks are accomplished by showing rich, interactive graphics that can be personalized for each user.

Main Takeaways:

  • How global warming is perceived today.
  • Share specific data on CO2 and warming trends.
  • How global warming can impact them where they live

Who are the Users?

Anyone who lives in the United States and are neutral or passive in how global warming impacts them and their community.

Datasets

  • US Greenhouse Gas Data
  • US Oil & Gas Well Data
  • US Carbon Dioxide Data by State
  • Global Warming Perception - US
  • Climate Change: Disaster and Financial Impact
